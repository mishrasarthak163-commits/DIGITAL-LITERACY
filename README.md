# 💻 Digital Literacy

## 📘 Description
Digital literacy is the ability to use digital devices, the internet, and online tools effectively and responsibly to find, understand, create, and share information. It also involves evaluating online content and maintaining safety while using technology.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Understand the concept of digital literacy  
- Learn how to use digital tools effectively  
- Promote safe and responsible internet usage  

---

## 🧠 Key Points
- Ability to search and evaluate information  
- Effective communication using digital platforms  
- Awareness of online safety and privacy  
- Basic technical knowledge of devices and software  

---

## 🛡️ Safe Practices
- Use strong passwords  
- Avoid sharing personal information  
- Be cautious of suspicious links  
- Keep software updated  

---

## 📌 Conclusion
Digital literacy is an essential skill in today’s world. It helps individuals use technology efficiently, stay safe online, and make informed decisions.
